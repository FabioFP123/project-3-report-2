Welcome to Cosmos Breaker!

Cosmos Breaker is a web game inspired by the hit retro video game, Breakout. The aim of the game is to destroy each of the "bricks" in your current level to progress to the next level.

Use the space key to launch the ball forwards, and the directional arrows to move the paddle Remember, if you run out of lives, you have to restart your progress!.
